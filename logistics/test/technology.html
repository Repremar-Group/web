Why Choose us?

Here are a few reasons why our customers choose our platform.

  • Easy Usage

    This platform ensures quick onboarding and minimal training, allowing companies to efficiently manage all the shipments data without technical expertise. This user-friendly interface increases adoption rates and streamlines daily operations, saving time and reducing errors.

  • Tracking

    Using our platform for cargo tracking provides real-time visibility and updates, enhancing efficiency and reducing delays. It also streamlines communication and documentation, leading to improved accuracy and customer satisfaction.

  • Reduce Costs

    Our technology help to reduces costs by minimizing delays, preventing lost shipments, and optimizing route planning. Clients can avoid unnecessary expenses, such as demurrage fees or expedited shipping, by having real-time insights into their cargo's location and status.

How It Works?

Here are the 3 working steps on success.

Secure Tracking

The platform connects with shipping lines through APIs, retrieving real-time GPS data from vessels to display the container's current location.

Updates

The platform updates dates by continuously syncing with the shipping line's systems, recalculating estimated arrival times based on real-time tracking data and any delays or progress detected in the container's journey.

Always up to date

The platform notifies customers of date changes by sending automated alerts via email, SMS, Whatsapp or in-app notifications, triggered whenever real-time tracking data indicates a significant update in the container's estimated arrival time.
