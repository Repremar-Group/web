REPREMAR Logistics / ABOUT US


Originally conceived in 1996 as a division of REPREMAR Shipping to develop air cargo, ocean consolidated, and logistic services, REPREMAR Logistics became independent on May 1, 1999.
Since then, REPREMAR Logistics has experienced an extraordinary development based on its dynamic management, its qualified and professional staff with a strong sense of duty, and its top-level network of global agents and representatives.
In an increasingly complex, dynamic, and demanding world, we are totally convinced of the importance of an Information Technology System. With this in mind, we are continuously investing on IT equipment and software to enhance and strengthen our abilities.
1 2 3

Part of REPREMAR Group

We are part of a Group of Companies offering ship agency, port operations, international cargo transportation and integral logistics services. Since its establishment in 1946, REPREMAR Shipping, our parent company, has been one of the most well-known, efficient, and traditional Shipping Agencies within the region.


Services

LOGISTICS AND TRANSPORTATION / SERVICES


REPREMAR Logistics offers the most complete logistics and transportation service mix: Ocean Freights, Full & Consolidated,
Air Freights, Inland Freights, Warehousing, Ports, Free Trade Zones, Cargo Insurance, Project Cargo and Special Services

INLAND FREIGHTS

Regarding domestic transport, we coordinate and pick up the cargo, in the case of exports, or deliver it, in the case of imports, from / at the address indicated by the client.
Our service range covers Full Container Load, LCL, FTL and LTL. For international transport, we have signed important agreements with international road transportation companies, which guarantees a wide range of possibilities, always optimizing costs, and with our added value: constant coordination and cargo tracking.

AIR FREIGHTS

All destinations and all origins covered with the invaluable support from our Operations Center at Carrasco International Airport in Montevideo. The fact of being the representatives of one of the most important agents in air transport guarantees reliability for the transport of your cargoes. Our expertise makes possible the proper handling of hazardous cargoes, perishable goods, fragile cargoes and living animals. We offer our services and advice for the handling and packaging of all kinds of goods.

OCEAN FREIGHTS,
FULL & CONSOLIDATED

We have a reliable network of experienced international agents for the coordination of our ocean shipments, both for FCL and LCL cargo which allows us to participate in most ocean services, being market leader in many of them.

WAREHOUSING,
PORTS & FREE TRADE ZONES

We have excellent warehouses inside the port premises, under the Montevideo Free Port regime, which facilitates the movement of cargo in transit. Outside port facilities, we can offer two options: the container terminal, where we provide a complete range of logistics services; and warehouses inside Montevideo, Florida, and Colonia Free Trade Zones.

CARGO INSURANCE

We offer the best insurance coverage for intermodal transportation. This service is provided by Aktiv-Assekuranz.

PROJECT CARGO

We ship cargo from suppliers around the globe and we deliver it at construction sites in any part of the world. We handle any kind of shipment that might be part of a project, from LCLs and FCLs to break-bulk parts needing special handling. Our staff has experts in vessel chartering, heavy-load handling, trucking and project management. The evidence relies in having successfully accomplished the largest logistical project and investment in Uruguay’s history.

LOGISTICS FOR
AGRO INDUSTRIES

Due to the new technological and logistical challenges that have arisen in the 21st century for the agribusiness, we are now providing logistics and innovative solutions for the agricultural industries. From machinery imports to commodities exports, REPREMAR Logistics covers the whole range of necessary services for today’s rural producers and agricultural companies.
