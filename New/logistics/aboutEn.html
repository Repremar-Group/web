REPREMAR LOGISTICS

A Repremar Group Company.

Who we are?

Originally conceived in 1996 as a division of REPREMAR Shipping to develop air cargo, consolidated sea freights and logistics services, Repremar Logistics became independent on May 1st, 1999.

Since then, Repremar Logistics gone through a huge development basen on a dynamic vision, capable and professional personnel, commited on mantaining the best possible service. With the support of a global network of first-class agents and representations.

  • Quiality certified.
  • We offer the best insurance on intermodal transport.
  • Continuously investing in hardware and software to reinforce our capabilities.
  • With headquarters in Uruguay and offices in Paraguay, we plan to continue expanding on the region.

Our Group

Part of Repremar Group.

REPREMAR is a Group of leading companies that offers maritime agency services, international cargo transportation, port operations, comprehensive logistics services, cargo GSA for airlines, real estate and special projects. Our mother company, REPREMAR Shipping is a renowned maritime agency that offers back-office services for cruise ships, container ships, oil tankers, bulk carriers and ship links that operate in any port in the region.

Repremar Group

We are a group of companies mainly related to foreign trade, our parent company Repremar Shipping was founded in 1946, it formerly belonged to the Vestey Group (England). In 1982 it was acquired by the Pena family. With the new owners, the company was reorganized, achieving a great expansion in its activities and the scope of its services.

Who does repremar Group represent?

We represent some of the leading shipping companies in the world, such us COSCO, CHINA SHIPPING and CSAV which trust repremar Shipping as their regional partner, stablishing joint-ventures on Uruguay and Argentina, in 1993, 2005 y 2008 respectively.
